How to Play

🎯 Goal

Slide tiles on the board to combine matching numbers. Reach the 2048 tile to win!

🕹️ Controls

Use arrow keys or swipe on mobile to move all tiles in that direction.

↑ ↓ ← → Swipe

🔢 Merging

When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one — doubling the value and adding to your score.

💡 Tips

Keep your highest tile in a corner. Build a chain of descending values along the edges. Don't let the board fill up!
