📖 How to Play Roulette

Goal

Predict where the ball will land and place your bets.

Betting Options

  • RED/BLACK — Bet on color (pays 2:1)
  • EVEN/ODD — Bet on even/odd numbers (pays 2:1)
  • 1-18 / 19-36 — Bet on number range (pays 2:1)

How to Play

  1. Select a chip value
  2. Click bet buttons to place chips
  3. Click SPIN to start
  4. Win 2× your bet on winning bets!

Quick Actions

  • ½ Bank — Bet half your balance
  • All In 🔥 — Bet everything
